Governance

The system of corporate governance adopted by Telecom Italia, articulated according to the traditional method, is focused on the role of leadership in the strategic decision taken by the Board of Directors.

We are convinced that a good corporate governance can and has to always identify new margins for improvement, in light of external stimuli and constraints, as well as application experience.
