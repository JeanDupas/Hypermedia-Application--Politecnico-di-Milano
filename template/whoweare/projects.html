On September 25, representatives of the 193 UN member states will commit to 17 Global Goals: macro-objectives for the next 15 years, to put an end to hunger and poverty, inequality and injustice, overfishing and climate change. A mission from which depends the quality of life of future generations, the objectives that need the active contribution of all. TIM, along with other mobile operators and media from around the world, supports the campaign to disseminate the Global Goals: 7 billion people in 7 days to say "It's time to change the world".
